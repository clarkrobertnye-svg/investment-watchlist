üìä Key Metrics

VCR (Value Creation Ratio)
ROIC / WACC. Earning above cost of capital signals competitive advantage. Must be ‚â•1.0.
EP Spread (Economic Profit)
ROIC - WACC. Creating economic value by generating returns above funding costs.

Direction of capital efficiency. Current ROIC vs 3Y Average.
Gross Margin
Revenue remaining after direct costs. Indicates pricing power.
Growth
3Y Historical Revenue CAGR.
IRR (Implied Return Rate)
Yield + Sustainable Growth. Expected 1Y total return.
