TVCR — Total Value Creation Rate
Power + Capital Return Yield. Our unified metric: how fast is intrinsic value compounding? ≥20% required (Gate 2).
Power — Reinvestment Engine
ROIIC × Reinvestment Rate. Value created by deploying capital into the business at high returns.
CRYld — Capital Return Yield
Buybacks + Dividends as % of beginning invested capital. Value returned to shareholders directly.
ROIC — Return on Invested Capital
NOPAT / Invested Capital. 5-year average. Measures efficiency of all capital deployed. ≥12% avg or ≥18% current (Gate 1).
ROIIC — Incremental ROIC
ΔNOPAT / ΔInvested Capital. Return on NEW capital invested. Capped at 100%. Drives future compounding.
PS CAGR — Per-Share Growth
Best of NOPAT/sh or FCF/sh 5yr CAGR (3yr fallback). Proof the engine is translating to per-share value. ≥15% required (Gate 3).
SBC-adj FCF CAGR
FCF/sh growth after deducting stock-based compensation. The real owner cash flow growth rate.
FCF/NI — Cash Conversion
Free Cash Flow / Net Income. ≥70% required (Gate 4). Ensures reported earnings convert to real cash.
ND/EBITDA — Leverage
Net Debt / EBITDA. ≤2.5× required (Gate 4). Negative = net cash position.
SBC/FCF — Stock Comp Haircut
Stock-based compensation as % of free cash flow. ≤35% required (Gate 4). Higher = more FCF is phantom, not owner cash.
