## THE QUESTION

Why are stocks with:
- **Zero compounding power** (not growing intrinsic value)
- **Low VCR (<2x)** (barely creating value above cost of capital)

---

## FRAMEWORK FOR EVALUATION

A stock should STAY if:
1. **High VCR (3x+)** = Strong moat, returning excess capital is rational
2. **High ROIC (25%+)** = Exceptional business, just mature
3. **Special circumstances** = Financial services, unique business model

A stock should GO if:
1. **Low VCR (<2x)** + **Low ROIC (<20%)** = Weak moat, weak returns
2. **Commodity-like business** = No pricing power
3. **Declining fundamentals** = Moat eroding
